PLACEMENTS

The Training & Placement Cell interacts frequently with students and helps them realize their skill areas. The TPC implements custom made training programmes in soft skill and personality development. Special coaching in communicative English is given to students and the cell also arranges mock interviews and group discussions. Beaverly boasts of excellent placements ever since its inception. The Beaverly Corporate Relations and Placements team employ all modern methodologies to deliver talent that satisfies the requirements of the most demanding national and international recruiters.

Beaverly alumni are working in 200 plus marquee companies spread across the globe. Our placement policy aims at placing students with quality recruiters where they will be able to build a successful career. We do not entertain sub-standard recruiters for inflating the placement statistics. Our placement statistics are ethically clean. The year 2022 will be a memorable year for Beaverly as far as campus placements are concerned. We saw our students getting placed with higher salary packages in globally reputed companies. We saw global giants like IBM, PWC, EY, TCS, Infosys etc recruiting our students in 2022. This year also witnessed average salary moving up by 34%. Total offers are at 555 and our students who are graduating in 2022 got opportunity to attend recruitment drives from 93 companies till date.

OBJECTIVE :

  • To provide job opportunities by bringing the companies for campus recruitment to all the eligible students of final year.
  • To enhance current technical and practical knowledge of students
  • To make the students ‘PLUG & PLAY’ by imparting soft skills and employability skills.
  • To have strong Industry-Institute Interaction.
